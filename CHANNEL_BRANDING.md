# Channel Branding Guide

## Channel Name
**Depths** — short, evocative of depth/mystery

## Banner Design
- **Color palette**: Dark backgrounds (#0a0a0f to #1a1a2e) with red accents (#c0392b, #e74c3c)
- **Text**: Channel name in bold serif font
- **Subtitle**: "Where History Meets Mystery"
- **Schedule**: Daily Shorts + Weekly Long-Form

## Logo Design
- **Style**: "Depths" in gold text on dark gradient circle with ring accents
- **Colors**: Gold text (#ffd700), dark background, red accent rings

## Thumbnail Template
- **Size**: 1280×720 px
- **Style**: 3×2 photo grid with dark overlays
- **Text overlay**: Bold English title at bottom, centered, with shadow
- **Watermark**: "Depths" at bottom-left corner

## Upload Schedule
- **Daily Short**: Upload at 3PM Mecca time (12PM UTC)
- **Weekly Long-Form**: Upload Saturday at 4PM Mecca time (1PM UTC)
