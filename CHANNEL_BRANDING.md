# Channel Branding Guide

## Channel Name Suggestions (Arabic)
Based on the dark explainer niche, here are name suggestions:

1. **سردية** (Sardiyya) — "Narrative" — short, mysterious 
2. **ظل التاريخ** (Zill al-Tarikh) — "Shadow of History" — dark, historical   
3. **عقول مظلمة** (Uqool Muthlima) — "Dark Minds" — psychological angle
4. **خلف الحقيقة** (Khalfa al-Haqiqa) — "Behind the Truth" — conspiracy/mystery
5. **السرد المظلم** (Al-Sard al-Muthlim) — "The Dark Narrative" — matches niche

## Banner Design
- **Color palette**: Dark backgrounds (#0a0a0f to #1a1a2e) with red/orange accents (#c0392b, #e74c3c)
- **Style**: Similar to [CHANNEL_B]'s red/orange gradient banner
- **Text**: Channel name in Arabic calligraphy font (e.g., "Noto Naskh Arabic" or "Amiri")
- **Subtitle**: "تاريخ | حوادث | تحليل نفسي" (History | Incidents | Psychological Analysis)

## Logo Design
- **Style**: Minimalist icon — a stylized letter or symbol
- **Options**: 
  - عين (Eye) symbol — watching/observing
  - ظل (Shadow) silhouette 
  - حرف (First letter of channel name) in dramatic style
- **Colors**: Single color on transparent background (white or red)

## Thumbnail Template (PSD/guidelines)
- **Size**: 1280×720 px
- **Style**: 3×2 photo grid with dark overlays
- **Text overlay**: Bold Arabic title at bottom, centered, with shadow
- **Logo**: Small watermark in top-right corner
- **Inspiration**: [CHANNEL_A] thumbnail collage style
